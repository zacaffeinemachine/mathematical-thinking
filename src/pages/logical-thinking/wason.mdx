Four cards lie on a table. Every card has a **colour** on one side —
red or blue — and a **digit** on the other. Of the four cards, two
are lying colour-up and two are lying digit-up.

A friend now makes a claim about these four cards:

> *If a card is red on one side, then it has an even number on the
> other side.*

Your job is to decide whether the claim is true or false for the
cards as they lie. You may turn over as many cards as you like, but
turning a card takes effort, so you want to turn over *as few as
possible*.

**Which cards must you turn over to be absolutely certain whether
the claim is true or false?**

Click the cards you believe need to be flipped, then press **Check**.
